Who We Are

Organic Green Thumb TV is dedicated to empowering Ohio residents to cultivate their own cannabis gardens safely and legally. We believe in organic methods that produce high-quality, clean, and potent plants. Our founder is a certified expert with years of experience in hydroponics, soil cultivation, and pest management.

Our mission is to simplify the growing process and provide you with the knowledge to succeed. We focus on a hands-on, personalized approach to ensure you get the most out of your growing journey.
